Acme Corp Employee Handbook (Sample)

Paid Time Off (PTO)
Full-time employees receive 20 days of paid time off per calendar year. Unused PTO may carry over up to 5 days into the next year. Requests must be submitted in the HR portal at least 7 days in advance.

Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval. Core collaboration hours are 11:00–16:00 local time. Company-issued laptops must use VPN when accessing internal systems.

Benefits Enrollment
Medical, dental, and vision benefits open during the first 30 days of employment and again each November for the following calendar year. Dependents can be added within 30 days of a qualifying life event.

Expense Reimbursement
Business travel expenses are reimbursed within 10 business days after approval. Receipts are required for any expense over $25. Meals are capped at $60 per day unless pre-approved for client entertainment.
